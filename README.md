# As Is

**What you see is what you get. No refunds. No crying.**

As Is is a raw, chaotic, no-bullshit marketplace where everything is sold **strictly As Is**.

---

## Core Concept

A blunt, adult-oriented marketplace platform with minimal hand-holding. Built for people who can make decisions and live with them.

**Main Tagline:** What you see is what you get. No refunds. No crying.

## Fees

- **Listing Fee:** $0.20
- **Final Value Fee:** 2%
- **Juice:** $0.99
- **Promotional Spotlight:** $9.99 (7 days) / $19.99 (monthly)
- **Tips:** Sellers keep **100%**
- Sales tax is automatically collected and remitted by the platform

---

## Payouts

Payouts are triggered by **tracking showing "Delivered"**, not buyer confirmation.

- **New & Up & Coming Sellers:** 48 hours after delivery
- **Trusted Seller:** 24 hours after delivery
- **Top Seller:** Instant after delivery
- All sellers can choose **Weekly Payouts** instead
- Tips are paid out immediately

## Live Selling Rules

- 3 moderation levels: **Relaxed** (default), Medium, Strict
- Must show the item on camera during live
- No nudity or sexually explicit behavior (including background)
- Live games are allowed (Giveaways, Mystery Boxes, Spin the Wheel, etc.)
- All live sales are still **As Is**
