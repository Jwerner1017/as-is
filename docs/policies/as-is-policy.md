# As Is Policy

All items on As Is are sold **strictly As Is, Where Is, With All Faults**.

Buyers have no right to returns, refunds, or chargebacks for condition, missing parts, authenticity, defects, or “I changed my mind.”

**Only 3 Exceptions** where the platform will intervene:
1. The package never arrives according to tracking
2. The package arrives clearly empty (based on shipping weight)
3. The buyer receives a completely different category of item than what was listed

Sellers must provide honest, detailed descriptions and clear photos. Misrepresenting an item will result in permanent ban and forfeiture of funds.
