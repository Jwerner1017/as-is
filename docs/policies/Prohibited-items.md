# Prohibited Items

The following items are strictly prohibited on As Is:

- Alcohol or tobacco products
- Live animals or insects
- Human remains, body parts, or bodily fluids
- Pornography or sexually explicit items
- Real estate or vehicles
- Hazardous materials or chemicals
- Prescription drugs or street drugs
- Lottery tickets or gambling-related items
- Firearms and ammunition

**Counterfeit & Replica Policy**
All items must be authentic. Counterfeit goods are banned. 
Replicas must be clearly labeled **"REPLICA"** in the title and in photos.
