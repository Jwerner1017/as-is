# Juice Feature Specification

## Overview
Juice allows Trusted Sellers and above to boost their listing to the top of the homepage for 24 hours.

## Pricing & Limits
- Cost: $0.99 per use
- Duration: 24 hours from activation
- Can only be used **once every 24 hours** per listing
- Maximum of **2 Juiced listings per category** at the same time per seller

## How It Works
- When a seller activates Juice, the listing is placed in a special "Juiced" section at the top of the homepage
- Newer Juice activations push older ones down in the stack (rolling system)
- Juiced listings are visible to all users on the main feed

## Restrictions
- Only available to Trusted Sellers and Top Sellers
- Cannot be used on ended or sold listings
