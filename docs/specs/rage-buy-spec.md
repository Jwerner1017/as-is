# Rage Buy & All Mine Specification

## Triggering Rage Buy
- Automatically triggers after 15 total bids on a listing, **or**
- Seller can manually trigger it at any time during a live

## Pricing
- Rage Buy Price = Current highest bid + 20%
- All Mine Price = Original highest bid (before Rage Buy was triggered) + 25%

## Flow
1. Rage Buy button appears
2. First person to click pays current bid + 20% and wins
3. 3-second countdown immediately starts
4. During countdown, "ALL MINE" button appears
5. Anyone can click All Mine to steal it for original bid + 25%
6. After 3 seconds, if no one clicks All Mine, the Rage Buy winner keeps the item

## Important Rules
- Both Rage Buy and All Mine are final. No refunds or disputes allowed
- This feature is **Live Selling only**
